## Fees

Again. takes a **10% selling fee** plus a 3% buyer protection charge added
to the purchase price. Listing is free. We never charge sellers up front.

Shipping is paid by the buyer at a fixed rate based on parcel size:

- Small (most dresses): £3.95
- Medium: £5.95
- Large: £7.95

These cover Royal Mail Tracked 48. If the seller chooses a more expensive
carrier, they cover the difference themselves.

## Payments and payouts

Payments are processed by Stripe. Sellers must complete Stripe Connect
onboarding before their first listing can be published. Funds are released
to the seller's Stripe balance once the buyer protection window has closed
(48 hours after delivery, or earlier if the buyer confirms received).

Stripe holds funds for a few business days before depositing them in the
seller's linked bank account.

## Buyer protection

You have **48 hours** from delivery to open a return through the app. After
that, the order auto-completes and funds release to the seller.

If you open a return:

- The seller has 48 hours to accept or dispute.
- If accepted, you have 7 days to ship the item back with a tracked service.
- Once the seller confirms receipt (or 7 days after dispatch, whichever
  comes first), Stripe refunds the order.
- If the seller disputes, we step in and resolve manually.
