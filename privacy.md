# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 13 May 2026

Again. ("we", "us") operates the Again. mobile app and platform. This page
explains what personal data we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

We're based in the United Kingdom and act as a data controller under the
UK GDPR.

## What we collect

When you sign up and use the app:

- **Account details** — your email address and chosen display name.
- **Body measurements** — your UK dress size, plus optionally your height,
  weight, body shape, and bust/waist/hips. Used only to match you with
  dresses likely to fit. Never shown to other users.
- **Shipping address** — street address, postcode, phone number — collected
  before your first purchase or sale. Shared with carriers (Royal Mail,
  Evri, DPD via ShipEngine) so they can deliver the parcel.
- **Payment information** — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see, store
  or transmit your card details. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant; their privacy
  notice is at https://stripe.com/gb/privacy.
- **Listings and orders** — what you list for sale, what you buy or sell,
  reviews you write.
- **Messages** — text and images sent through the Again. inbox.
- **Photos** — listing photos you upload, plus any photos you attach to
  messages.
- **Device info** — your iOS version, app version, and a push notification
  token if you grant permission.
- **Crash and error logs** — captured by Sentry, an error-tracking service.
  Includes the screen you were on, the action you took, and your account
  ID at the moment of the crash. No card details or message contents.

We do **not** collect or sell your data for advertising. We do **not** use
your location for tracking; we ask for it only to suggest a city for local
pickup, and only when you've granted permission.

## Why we collect it

Specifically:

- **Email + display name** — so we can identify your account.
- **Size profile + measurements** — to power the match algorithm. This is
  the whole point of the app.
- **Shipping address + phone** — to print a label that gets the parcel to
  you. Without this, posted orders can't be fulfilled.
- **Payment data via Stripe** — to process the purchase and pay the seller.
- **Listings, orders, reviews, messages** — to operate the marketplace.
- **Push token** — to notify you when a buyer messages you or your order
  ships. You can turn these off in Profile → Notifications.
- **Crash logs** — to find and fix bugs that affect you.

The legal basis for processing is: **contract** (to provide the service you
signed up for) for almost all of the above, and **legitimate interests** for
crash logging and basic analytics.

## Who we share data with

- **Stripe** — to process payments and route money to sellers.
- **ShipEngine + Royal Mail / Evri / DPD** — to produce shipping labels.
  They receive the buyer's name, address, phone and parcel weight.
- **Sentry** — to receive crash reports.
- **Firebase (Google)** — our hosting infrastructure (database, auth, file
  storage, push delivery). Data is held in Google's UK / EU regions.

We do not share or sell data to any other third party. We do not use your
data for advertising or pass it to data brokers.

## How long we keep it

- **Account data** — until you delete your account. Account deletion is
  available in-app: Profile → Delete account.
- **Orders and reviews** — kept indefinitely after account deletion as
  anonymous records, because we're required to retain transaction records
  for accounting and dispute resolution. Your display name is removed but
  the order amount, date and counterparty record remain.
- **Messages** — deleted when either party deletes their account.
- **Crash logs in Sentry** — 90 days, then automatically purged.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can:

- **Access** the data we hold on you.
- **Correct** anything that's wrong.
- **Delete** your account and most associated data (subject to the
  transaction-records exception above).
- **Export** your data in a portable format.
- **Restrict or object** to specific kinds of processing.
- **Withdraw consent** for push notifications and other optional things at
  any time.

To exercise any of these, email **hello@again.app**. We'll respond within
30 days as required by law.

If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you can also complain
to the UK Information Commissioner's Office: https://ico.org.uk/concerns.

## Cookies and tracking

The app doesn't use cookies (it isn't a website). We don't track you across
other apps or websites. We don't use the iOS Identifier for Advertisers
(IDFA).

## Children

Again. is not for children. You must be 18 or over to use the app.

## Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll tell you in the app and update the
"Last updated" date above.
